# Privacy Policy for ScanMaster - QR Scanner

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

ScanMaster - QR Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **QR Code and Barcode Data**: When you scan QR codes or barcodes, the content is stored locally on your device in the scan history. This data is only stored on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
- **Generated QR Codes**: When you generate QR codes, the input data (text, URLs, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, contact information) is processed locally on your device to create QR code images.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect certain information automatically, including:
  - Device type and model
  - Operating system version
  - App version
  - Unique device identifiers
  - IP address

### 3. Advertising Data
- Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
  - Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or iOS Identifier for Advertisers)
  - Location data (approximate location based on IP address)
  - App usage data
  - Interaction data with advertisements

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **App Functionality**: To provide QR code and barcode scanning services, generate QR codes, and maintain your scan history locally on your device.

2. **Advertising**: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob uses collected data to show personalized ads and measure ad performance.

3. **App Improvement**: To analyze app usage patterns and improve user experience.

4. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- **Scan History**: All scanned QR codes and barcodes are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.
- **Generated QR Codes**: Generated QR code images can be saved to your device's storage or shared through your device's sharing functionality.

### Data Retention
- Scan history is stored locally until you manually delete it using the delete function in the App.
- You can clear all history at any time using the "Clear All" option in the History page.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
Our App uses Google AdMob for advertising services. AdMob may collect and use information according to their Privacy Policy:
- **Google Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **AdMob Policy**: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

AdMob may use:
- Device identifiers for ad personalization
- Location data (approximate)
- App activity data
- Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's ad settings:
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

## Permissions

Our App requests the following permissions:

1. **Camera Permission**: Required to scan QR codes and barcodes using your device's camera.
2. **Storage Permission** (Android): Required to save generated QR code images to your device's gallery.
3. **Photo Library Permission** (iOS): Required to save generated QR code images to your device's photo library.

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality and are not used to collect additional personal information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

1. **Advertising Partners**: We share data with Google AdMob for advertising purposes as described above.

2. **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

3. **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

## Your Rights

1. **Access**: Request access to your personal data.
2. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data.
3. **Opt-Out**: Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.
