How to Request Data Deletion

At ScanMaster - QR Scanner, we respect your privacy and provide easy ways to delete your data. You can request data deletion through the following methods:

Method 1: Delete Data Directly from the App (Recommended)

For Scan History (Local Data):

  1. Open ScanMaster - QR Scanner on your device
  2. Go to the "History" tab (bottom navigation)
  3. To delete individual items: Tap on a history item → Tap the "Delete" button
  4. To delete all history: Tap the "Clear All" button (trash icon) in the top right corner
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Note: This will immediately and permanently delete all scan history stored locally on your device.

Method 2: Contact Us via Email

If you need assistance or want to request deletion of any other data, please contact us:

Email: dhuhastudio26@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - ScanMaster - QR Scanner

Please include in your email:

  • Your request to delete data
  • Your device information (optional, for verification purposes)
  • Any specific data you want deleted

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm the deletion.

Method 3: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling ScanMaster - QR Scanner will:

  • Delete all locally stored scan history
  • Remove the app and all its data from your device

Note: Data collected by Google AdMob (for advertising) may still be retained by Google according to their privacy policy. See "AdMob Data" section below.

Types of Data and Deletion Information

1. Scan History (Local Storage)

What is stored:

  • QR code and barcode content you scanned
  • Scan timestamp
  • Barcode format/type

Storage location: Local device only (SharedPreferences)

Deletion: ✅ Immediately deleted when you use "Clear All" or delete individual items

Retention period: Data is stored until you manually delete it. No automatic deletion.

Can be deleted: Yes, completely and permanently

2. Generated QR Code Data

What is stored:

  • Data you entered to generate QR codes (text, URLs, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, contacts)
  • Generated QR code images (if saved to gallery)

Storage location: Local device only

Deletion: ✅ Immediately deleted when you delete the app or clear app data

Retention period: Stored until app uninstallation or manual deletion

Can be deleted: Yes, completely and permanently

Note: If you saved QR code images to your device gallery, you need to delete them manually from your gallery app.

3. Google AdMob Data (Third-Party)

Important: This data is collected and managed by Google AdMob, not by ScanMaster - QR Scanner directly.

What is collected:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • App usage data
  • Ad interaction data

Storage location: Google's servers

Deletion by developer: ❌ We cannot delete this data as it is managed by Google

What you can do:

  1. Opt-out of personalized ads:
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  2. Reset Advertising ID:
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Reset Advertising Identifier
  3. Review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Retention period: Governed by Google's privacy policy and data retention practices

4. Device Information

What may be collected:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address (for AdMob)

Storage location: Used for app functionality and AdMob services

Deletion: Automatically cleared when app is uninstalled

Retention period: Only during app usage

Data Retention Summary

Data Type Retention Period Can Be Deleted
Scan History Until manually deleted ✅ Yes (from app)
Generated QR Data Until app uninstall ✅ Yes (uninstall app)
AdMob Data Per Google's policy ⚠️ Opt-out only (via device settings)

Additional Information

Response Time: We will process data deletion requests within 30 days of receipt.

Verification: We may request verification to ensure the request is legitimate and to protect your privacy.

Confirmation: After processing your request, we will confirm the deletion via email.
